Fighter Backstory

Andy Krail got his nickname because “later” has never survived in his vocabulary. Need someone to jump first, chase first, bleed first-Andy is already moving before the order is finished. It is equal parts courage and a spectacular lack of self-preservation.

He earned his local legend during a convoy mess when a sump crawler took his dropped chainsword and half his good sense with it. Andy jumped after the weapon, came back burned, bleeding, laughing, and somehow still holding the blade. Hank called it idiotic. Isaac called it useful idiocy if controlled. Grinnarg called it potential with a very short fuse.

In the Black Luggers’ command ladder, Andy is a prospect: low rank, high supervision, permanent scrutiny. Hank keeps him on a leash he pretends not to notice. Isaac punishes overextensions like they are a personal insult. Tyra keeps assigning him jobs that reward speed without rewarding chaos. Karl and Raphael have both dragged him back behind cover often enough to charge rent.

His closest mirrors are the other prospects. Charly can shut him up with one look when he gets stupid. Barnabas eggs him on in exactly the wrong moments. Joy scares him a little because she predicts his mistakes before he makes them-and then saves him from them anyway.

Under the bravado, Andy wants to belong more than he wants to be famous. Lately he checks corners, waits for signals, and admits when he blew a call. For him, that is growth. In the underhive, growth is another word for survival.
